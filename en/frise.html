  • Timeline
Marie Curie 1867-1934

Nobel Prize shared with Pierre Curie and Henri Becquerel for the discovery of radioactivity. Often presented as 'her husband’s assistant' before being fully recognized.

Lise Meitner 1878-1968

Work on nuclear fission attributed to Otto Hahn, the only one awarded the Nobel.

Rosalind Franklin 1920-1958

His X-ray diffraction images allowed the discovery of the structure of DNA. Credited after her death, her colleagues Watson and Crick received the Nobel.

Marthe Gautier 1925-2022

Discover the additional chromosome at the origin of trisomy 21, but the recognition goes to Jérôme Lejeune.

Jocelyn Bell Burnell 1943-

Discovers pulsars, but the 1974 Nobel Prize is awarded to his superiors.
